ABOUT US

Tortilla Flat is a family owned and operated business that provides patrons with fine family dining. We have a fully stocked bar and a lounge area with TVs for your entertainment. For over 40 years, we take pride in preparing homemade food from family recipes. We always use fresh, quality ingredients that are never frozen to ensure great flavor in every bite.
